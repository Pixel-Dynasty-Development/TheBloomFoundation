The Bloom Foundation is a private, nonprofit, locally run organization with the aim to decrease hunger and poverty in the Tennessee counties of Jefferson and Hamblen. We are committed to the mission of building brighter futures for children and families by providing essential resources and support. We believe every individual deserves the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential.

Our organization was founded on the principle that community support is vital. We work tirelessly to provide ongoing and updated local resources, coordinating with other community organizations and municipal agencies, and volunteer work to benefit those in need. Our initiatives are designed to meet a wide range of needs, from food assistance and educational resources, to family resource program information.

At The Bloom Foundation, we are committed to making a difference, one life and one family at a time. We are proud of the strong relationships we build and the positive impact we have on the lives of individuals in our community. Join us in our journey to provide hope and a new beginning for those facing hardship.
