About The Bloom Foundation

The Bloom Foundation is a private foundation dedicated to decreasing poverty and child hunger in Jefferson and Hamblen County. For over 15 years, we have been committed to our mission of building brighter futures for children and families by providing essential resources and support. We believe every individual deserves the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential.

Our organization was founded on the principle that community support is vital. We work tirelessly to provide comprehensive programs, ongoing resources, and dedicated assistance to those in need. Our initiatives are designed to meet a wide range of needs, from food assistance and educational support to family resource programs.

At The Bloom Foundation, we are committed to making a difference, one life and one family at a time. We are proud of the strong relationships we build and the positive impact we have on the lives of individuals in our community. Join us in our journey to provide hope and a new beginning for those facing hardship.

Our Vision

To create a community where poverty and child hunger are eradicated, and every individual has access to the resources and opportunities needed to lead a healthy, fulfilling life. We envision a society where compassion and support are at the forefront, fostering growth and well-being for all.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Approaching every individual and family with empathy, understanding, and a deep commitment to their well-being.
  • Integrity: Operating with transparency, honesty, and accountability in all our actions and decisions.
  • Empowerment: Providing individuals and families with the tools, resources, and support to overcome challenges and achieve their full potential.
  • Community: Building strong, supportive networks among community members, staff, and partners to create a sense of belonging and shared purpose.
  • Advocacy: Championing the rights and needs of those facing poverty and hunger, ensuring their voices are heard and their futures are protected.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team is comprised of passionate professionals who bring a wealth of experience and a deep commitment to our mission. From our program coordinators and social workers to our administrative staff and leadership, every member of The Bloom Foundation is united by a common goal: to provide the best possible outcomes for the individuals and families we serve. We are here to guide, support, and celebrate with you every step of the way.
